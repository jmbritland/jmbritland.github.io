About the AY27 site and scheduling changes:

The Office of Graduate Medical Education (GME) is implementing a structural change within MedHub that requires all programs to define one site to one service. When defining a new Service, the option to use “set multiple” sites will no longer be available. Current Services with multiple sites must be redefined effective Academic Year 2027 (AY27).

Additionally, all internal locations within the UUHC system will be re-classified in MedHub as one of the sites below:

The ARUP site will remain unchanged. The ARUP site abbreviation will remain as [ARUP].

The ARUP Laboratory Research site will remain unchanged. The ARUP Laboratory Research site abbreviation will remain as [ARUPLab].

The Huntsman Cancer Hospital site will be renamed to HCH / HCI Clinical. The HCH / HCI Clinical site abbreviation will be [HCH].

These sites will be consolidated under the Huntsman Cancer Hospital Laboratory Research site, which will be renamed to HCH / HCI Lab Research. The HCH / HCI Lab Research site abbreviation will be [HCHLab].
