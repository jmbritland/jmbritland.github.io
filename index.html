At the February 2026 GME Training Workshop, the Office of GME will be presenting on new site and scheduling practices for AY27. We want to gather your feedback now to help guide our training presentation and ensure the transition addresses your needs and challenges.

The Office of Graduate Medical Education (GME) is implementing a structural change within MedHub that requires all programs to define one site to one service. When defining a new Service, the option to use “set multiple” sites will no longer be available. Current Services with multiple sites must be redefined effective Academic Year 2027 (AY27).
